# College Recruitment Improvement Study
Non-majors study
The study conducted focused on finding out what major a student is in and what led them to take on their major. Along with specific questions about computer science courses. I believe the focus of this study was to find out what type of activities students were involved in prior to attending CCM and figuring out what led them to take on their specified major at CCM. 

Analysis.ipynb - Notebook containing answers to questions, and other conclusions made from the data
Non-Majors Survey Results - Initial dataset
Cleaned-2.ipynb - Notebook containing code used to clean data
df_q(1-5) - Cleaned data, different datasets created for q1, q2, etc.
